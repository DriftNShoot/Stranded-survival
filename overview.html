Storyline of Stranded Survival The Game

When you start you find yourself on an island which you have drifted to after your boat has broken down, YOU will need to search round the island finding the equipment to fix your boat and be able to escape this island.

BUT YOU WILL FIND OUT THAT THERE IS MORE TO THE ISLAND AND IT IS NOT AS UNHABITABLE AS YOU MAY OF THOUGHT
